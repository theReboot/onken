This research report, created for both investors and media actors, seeks to help establish a truly independent media sector in Kenya. Right now, deep-rooted challenges inhibit Kenyan journalists from holding their government accountable; new streams of funding are needed so that innovative, rigorous media outlets can gain a foothold. This report is a product of the Reboot Media program, which strengthens government watchdogs worldwide.
